omenum Revelio

Click on a square to open it

Within each square is either a number or a Death Eater

The number indicates how many Death Eaters are adjacent to that square (i.e. in the 8 squares surrounding it)

Right click on a square to place a House banner on it, thus marking it as a possible Death Eater (right click again to remove the banner)

If you click on a Death Eater, you lose the game

The goal is to open all non-Death Eater squares, and you win the game
